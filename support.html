Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a game's Universe ID?

1. Go to the game on Roblox website
2. Look at the URL - the number is the Place ID or Universe ID
3. Paste it in GameHub search

Why can't I find my game?

Make sure you're using the correct Universe ID or Place ID from the game's URL.

How do I remove games from my watchlist?

Tap the bookmark icon on any game to remove it from your watchlist.
