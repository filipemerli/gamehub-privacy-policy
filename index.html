1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Personal Data Collection

GameHub does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal details.

1.2 Local Data Storage

All data you create in the app is stored locally on your device:

  • Watchlist: Your saved Roblox games are stored in a local SQLite database on your device only
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences are stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your device

1.3 Public Roblox Data

GameHub retrieves publicly available data from Roblox's official public APIs to display game statistics. This data is:

  • Already publicly accessible on Roblox's platform
  • Fetched directly from Roblox servers (not stored on our servers)
  • Displayed to you in real-time
  • Never collected, stored, or transmitted by us

Examples of public data displayed: game player counts, ratings, descriptions, and other publicly visible game information.

1.4 External Links

GameHub provides convenient links to:

  • The official Roblox mobile app
  • Roblox game pages on the Roblox website

When you click these links, you will be directed to Roblox's platform, which is governed by Roblox's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

2. How the App Works

GameHub operates entirely on your device with no data collection:

  • Display Public Data: Fetches and displays publicly available Roblox game statistics in real-time
  • Local Watchlist: Stores your favorite games locally in a SQLite database on your device
  • Quick Links: Provides convenient links to open games in the Roblox app or website
  • Offline Functionality: Your watchlist remains accessible even without internet connection

No data is sent to our servers because we don't have any servers. The app communicates only with Roblox's public APIs to fetch game information.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 No Data Collection = No Data Sharing

Since GameHub does not collect any personal data, we have no data to share, sell, or disclose to third parties.

3.2 No Third-Party Services

GameHub does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any other third-party tracking or data collection services

3.3 Roblox API Usage

The app communicates directly with Roblox's public APIs to fetch game information. This communication:

  • Goes directly from your device to Roblox servers
  • Is subject to Roblox's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
  • Does not involve our servers or data collection

4. Data Retention and Deletion

All your data stays on your device. When you uninstall GameHub, all locally stored data (your watchlist and preferences) is automatically deleted from your device according to Android's standard app removal process.

You can also manually clear your watchlist at any time through the app's interface.

5. Your Rights and Control

5.1 Complete Control Over Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • View Your Data: All your watchlist data is visible within the app
  • Modify Your Data: Add or remove games from your watchlist anytime
  • Delete Your Data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Export Not Needed: Your data never leaves your device, so there's nothing to export from our servers

5.2 No Opt-Out Required

Since GameHub doesn't include tracking, analytics, or advertisements, there's nothing to opt out of. The app is private by default.

6. Children's Privacy

Safe for All Ages: GameHub does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app:

  • Does not require registration or account creation
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or any identifying information
  • Does not include chat features or user-generated content
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Only displays publicly available Roblox game information

Parents can review what data is stored by simply opening the app - it's only the watchlist of Roblox games your child has saved.

7. Data Security

GameHub's privacy-first architecture provides inherent security benefits:

  • Local Storage Only: Your watchlist is stored in Android's secure SQLite database on your device
  • No Server Vulnerabilities: Since we don't collect data on servers, your information can't be exposed in a data breach
  • No Transmission Risk: Your personal data never travels over the internet
  • Standard Android Security: Your data benefits from Android's built-in app sandboxing and security features

The app uses HTTPS when communicating with Roblox's public APIs to ensure secure connections.

  • No Disclosure: We don't disclose personal information to third parties

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), GameHub's design provides strong privacy protections that align with GDPR principles:

  • Data Minimization: We collect zero personal data, exceeding GDPR's minimization requirement
  • Purpose Limitation: No data collection means no purposes to limit

  • Right to Access: View your watchlist anytime in the app
  • Right to Erasure: Uninstall the app to delete all local data

Since we don't process personal data, we don't require a legal basis for processing under GDPR.
